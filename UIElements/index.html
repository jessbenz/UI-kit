SIZES

These sizes are best for web apps. You may need to tweak for other applications:

Body 14px, line-height 1.6em

Caption 0.8em (apply 'small-text' class)

h3 sub heading 1.6em

h2 page title 2.4em

h1 misc 3em

(good for welcome message or a larger page title)
